How I Help

Many companies begin with a specific need — a brochure, website, presentation, or product message. Often, the deeper issue is strategic clarity: who the product is for, why it matters, and how it should be positioned in the market.

I help diagnose those questions first, then support strategy development and, when useful, help translate that strategy into clear commercial communications.
